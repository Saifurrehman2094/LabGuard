USAGE FLOW:
================================================================================

Teacher Creates Exam
  ↓
Opens Exam Creation Form
  ↓
Clicks "Import questions from a platform"
  ↓
Selects Platform (Codeforces | AtCoder | HackerRank)
  ↓
Configures Filters
  - Difficulty: Easy or Medium
  - Topics: Relevant subjects
  - Required Concepts: Optional
  - Count: 1-5 problems
  ↓
Clicks "🚀 Fetch and generate questions"
  ↓
Waits for Progress
  - Progress bar updates every stage change
  - May take 1-10 minutes depending on count
  ↓
Reviews Questions
  - Each shown as success card (green) or failure card (red)
  - Can add individually or batch add all successful
  ↓
Adds Questions to Exam
  - Immediately available in exam's question list
  - Can edit, add more test cases, or delete
  - Source attribution tracked in database
  ↓
Continues Exam Setup
  - Can add more questions manually
  - Can import from more platforms
  - Creates exam when ready
